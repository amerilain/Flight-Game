Introduction

Your Ship has Crash Landed!

You are an Alien who has been sent to Earth to research this planet's strange weather conditions.

Your ship has crashed in Helsinki, Finland but you must still continue to carry out your mission. Since you are on Earth, you will do as the Earthlings do and travel by plane.

Since you are aware of the disastrous impact c02 emissions can have on a planet's climate and your mission is commited to doing no harm, you have a limited supply of c02 to complete your task

Your task is as follows: Complete the eight listed weather goals by traveling to different locations where you may document the strange weather phenomenon. The goals are to experience:

  1. Temperature over 25°C
  2. Temperature under -20°C
  3. Temperature exactly 0°C
  4. Temperature exactly 10°C
  5. Temperature exactly 20°C
  6. CLear skies
  7. Cloudy conditions
  8. Wind that blows more than 10 m/s
